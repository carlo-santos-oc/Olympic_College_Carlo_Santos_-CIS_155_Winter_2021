Overview

In March of 2020 Covid-19 changed the way we live. The danger of the virus caused the world to shut down in a coordinated effort to stop the spread of sickness. With shut downs came the devastation of economies all over the world. We are closing in on a year of staying at home and avoiding large gatherings. But what is Covid-19? I hope to provide important information to help understand what the world has been up against and how to keep safe as we continue to deal with Covid-19 to this day.
